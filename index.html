Armaan Malik Biography


Armaan Malik is a prominent Indian singer and songwriter known for his soulful and melodious voice. He was born on July 22, 1995, in Mumbai, India, into a family of musicians. His father, Daboo Malik, is a music director, and his mother, Jyothi Malik, is a playback singer. Armaan comes from a musically inclined background, with his grandfather, Sardar Malik, being a noted music director in the Indian film industry.
Armaan developed a passion for music at a young age and started singing from the age of four. He received his formal training in music from the prestigious Berklee College of Music in Boston, Massachusetts. During his time at Berklee, he honed his skills and expanded his musical knowledge. Armaan Malik has received several accolades throughout his career, including Filmfare Awards, IIFA Awards, and Zee Cine Awards. He has also been nominated for prestigious awards such as the Stardust Awards and Screen Awards.
With his melodious voice, versatility, and captivating performances, Armaan Malik continues to enthrall music lovers in India and around the world.
